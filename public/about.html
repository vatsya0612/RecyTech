About RecyTech

A full-stack project designed to reduce electronic waste by turning unused devices into reusable components, resale value, and verified recycling supply.

Problem

People throw away electronics because they do not know the resale or recycling value. Repair shops struggle to source affordable old components.

Solution

RecyTech connects sellers, repair shops, refurbishers, and recyclers through verified listings, price estimates, and inquiries.

Impact

The platform encourages reuse before recycling, helps sellers earn real money, and supports responsible e-waste management in India.
